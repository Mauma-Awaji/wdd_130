Meet the Gym Leaders!

To become a Pokémon champion, you must first beat all 8 gym leaders and collect their gym's badges.
Hover above each gym leaders image to see what lies in store for you.
The first gym leader is Viola of Santalune City who uses bug types. Her team consists of 2 Pokémon. Her first is a female level 10 Surskit whose ability is swift swim. Her second and ace Pokemon is a female level 12 Vivillion with Shield Dust as its ability and an annoying move in Infestation that traps and deals damage every turn. Beating her earns you the Bug Badge.
Cyllage City’s Grant is the next leader who uses Rock types. His team has 2 pokemon. The first is a level 25 Amaura. Watch out for its Rock Tomb and Thunder Wave attacks that are sure to drop your speed. His ace is a lvl 25 Tyrunt with Strong Jaw that powers up its Bite attack. This earns you the Cliff Badge.
The 3rd leader is Korrina of Shalour City. Her team consists of 3 fighting types. First is an Inner Focus Mienfoo. Next is a Guts Machoke. Her ace is an Unburden Hawlucha with Flying Press, a move that is both a Flying and Fighting type move at the same time! Defeating her earns you the Rumble Badge.
Fourth is Ramos of Coumarine City. He has a team of 3 grass types. A Chlorophyll Jumpluff is first. Next is a Chlorophyll Weepinbell. His Ace is a Sap Sipper Gogoat with Grass Knot, Bulldoze and Take Down. Upon his defeat, he gives you the Plant Badge.
Clemont of Lumiose City is next with his team of 3 electric types. Static Emolga is first with Volt Switch for damaging switch-ins. Next is Sturdy Magneton. His ace is a Heliolisk that is immune to water attacks Thanks to its ability Dry Skin. The reward for this fight is the Voltage Badge.
Sixth is Valerie of Laverre City with a team of 3 fairy types. Hyper Cutter Mawile, Soundproof Mr. Mime and a Cute Charm Sylveon. She gives you the Fairy Badge for beating her.
The seventh gym leader is Olympia of Anistar City with her 3 psychic types. She has a Magic Guard Sigilyph, Oblivious Slowking and Infiltrator Meowstic that hits through screens and substitutes. The Psychic Badge is given for this victory.
Wulfric of Snowbelle City is the final gym leader with his team of 3 ice types. He has a Snow Warning Abomasnow that fires of priority Ice Shard along with his Levitate Cryogonal and his ace, Ice Body Avalugg. Upon his defeat, he gives you the Iceberg Badge.
